Scholarships

Student Travel Assistance Scholarship

Providing financial aid to students for travel expenses related to education. This scholarship aims to reduce the financial burden on students who need to travel for educational purposes, ensuring that they can focus on their studies without worrying about travel costs.

Research Scholarship

Supporting students in their research endeavors with financial assistance. This scholarship encourages and supports students in pursuing research projects by providing them with the necessary financial resources to conduct their studies effectively.

SUMMER INTERNSHIP ASSISTANCE

Providing support for students participating in summer internships. This scholarship helps students cover the costs associated with summer internships, allowing them to gain valuable work experience without financial constraints.

Awards

SURA Awards

Recognizing outstanding achievements in academia and research. The SURA Awards celebrate individuals who have made significant contributions to the fields of academia and research, highlighting their dedication and impact on the community.

Mantra Awards

Honoring excellence and dedication in the field of education. The Mantra Awards recognize individuals who have shown exceptional dedication and excellence in the field of education, inspiring others with their commitment to learning and growth.
