Mantra Awards

Honoring excellence and dedication in the field of education. The Mantra Awards recognize individuals who have shown exceptional dedication and excellence in the field of education, inspiring others with their commitment to learning and growth.

About the Award

  • The Center for Entrepreneurial Leadership (CEL) at BITS Pilani aims to create future leaders at BITS Pilani.
  • Recognize outstanding students with leadership potential and/or entrepreneurial initiative.
  • Four categories:
    •          Outstanding Leadership (1 male & 1 female) Rs. 5000 each
    •          Entrepreneur of the Year Rs. 5000
    •          Social Leadership Rs. 5000
    •          Innovator of the Year (optional award)
  • Open to all current, full-time students except past winners and those with disciplinary records.
  • More information and application process available on CEL website.

Application Process:

  • The Panel would call for nominations beforehand by electronic submission.
  • CEL should also proactively nominate deserving candidates.
  • CEL student body would be responsible for coordinating the awards.
  • Advertisements calling for nominations shall be put up on campus. They would also be marketed electronically, through email etc
  • Candidates submit a short write-up on their achievements electronically.
  • If required, more information would be requested from the candidates
