Bharati & B Balaji Scholarship

Mr. Balaji Bakthisaran from 1977-1982 Batch have initiated 03 Scholarship under 40% category of the Merit-Cum-Need (MCN) category for the qualified, needy, First-Degree students as per the scholarships policy applicable from time to time to study at the Pilani and Goa Campuses of BITS Pilani.

About the Scholarship

  • Donor(s): Mr. Balaji Bakthisaran (1977-1982 Batch)
  • Category: Merit-Cum-Need (MCN) 40%
  • Eligible Students: First-degree students in Core Engineering/Technical programs
  • Campus: Pilani & Goa
  • Award: 40% of tuition fees
  • Renewal: Based on maintaining 40% MCN criteria
  • Established: Academic Year 2021-22
